About Us

A&T Technologies specializes in computer and technology repair. We have more than a decade of experience working with consumer PC hardware, as well as more than 5 years of experience with enterprice networking, servers, and other equipment.

We have extensive experience with both consumer and enterprise network deployment and management, and have worked with many hardware vendors, including Dell, HP, and Supermicro.

History

We started small, and have had an interesting history along the way.

We originally started A&T Technologies by testing the waters with Android app development. We had some concepts for some games, and wanted to see if we could create something that people would enjoy, and that lived up to our expectations.

Some of these ideas ultimately never came to life, but for a few years, a couple of games were available on the Google Play store. Unfortunately, as this development was mostly a passion project alongside our other responsibilities, we weren't able to continue maintaining device support, and ultimately stopped developing this project.
